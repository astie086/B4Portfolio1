About me

My name is Asad Haider and I am a community organizer and web designer in Southern California.
I have been organizing since 2013 in fields ranging from labor to within queer, trans and
people of color communities. I am a painter, designer, developer and critical thinker.
Please check out my work below to see what I am passionate about.
